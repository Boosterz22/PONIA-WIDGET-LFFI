Quick Start

Integrate PONIA into your application in less than 1 minute. Just copy and paste this code into your HTML:

<iframe src="https://your-ponia.replit.dev/widget?chain=polygon" width="450" height="700" style="border: none; border-radius: 16px;" ></iframe>

That's it! Your users can now deposit from any supported blockchain to your platform's chain.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do transfers take?

Most transfers complete in 1-3 minutes. PONIA uses Across Protocol's intent-based relayer network for ultra-fast bridging.

Is PONIA safe to use?

Yes. PONIA is built on top of Across Protocol, which is production-tested and powers Uniswap's cross-chain bridging. All transactions are non-custodial and executed through audited smart contracts.

What wallets are supported?

PONIA uses Reown AppKit (formerly WalletConnect), supporting 300+ wallets including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, Ledger, Trezor, and more.

Can I customize the widget appearance?

The widget currently uses PONIA's default styling. Custom white-label themes are planned for future releases.

Do I need a backend to integrate PONIA?

No! PONIA is purely client-side. Just embed the iframe and you're done. The widget handles all wallet connections, chain switching, and transaction management.

What happens if a transfer fails?

If a transfer fails, funds remain in the user's wallet. No funds are lost. The user can retry the transaction or contact support.

How much does it cost to integrate?

Integration is 100% free. PONIA earns the 1.5% transaction fee, and you get seamless cross-chain payments without any upfront costs.
